JUDO – Survival

A competitive survival strategy board game built on permanent elimination, dynamic death tiles, shrinking zones, and last-player-standing victory.

✔ Dynamic Death Tiles

✔ Shrinking Playable Zones

Legends Mode — High-risk legendary tokens.
