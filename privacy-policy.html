Privacy Policy for JUDO – Survival

Effective Date: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the JUDO – Survival mobile application (hereby referred to as the “Application”) developed and published by Syed Abdul Kareem K (hereby referred to as the “Service Provider”).

The Application is provided as an Ad-Supported service and may include optional in-app purchases or premium features in future updates. This service is provided “AS IS.”

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when users interact with the Application.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application may collect certain information automatically when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Device Internet Protocol address (IP address)
  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Pages or screens visited within the Application
  • Time and date of usage
  • Time spent using the Application

This information is used to operate the Application, analyze performance, and improve the overall user experience.

2. Account Information

When using certain features of the Application, users may create or sign into an account through supported authentication methods. The Application may store limited account-related information such as:

  • User ID
  • Display name or username
  • Profile avatar
  • Authentication provider information

This information is used to manage user accounts, synchronize player progress, and enable multiplayer features.

3. Gameplay Data

To provide multiplayer functionality and game progression systems, the Application may collect gameplay-related information including:

  • Match results
  • Player rankings and statistics
  • In-game progress
  • In-game currency balances
  • Gameplay activity logs

This information helps maintain leaderboards, ranking systems, and fair gameplay.

4. Approximate Location Information

The Application may determine the approximate geographic region or country of users through IP-based location detection. This information is used for purposes such as:

  • Understanding player distribution by region
  • Improving server performance and matchmaking
  • Analyzing global gameplay trends
  • Enhancing game services and performance

The Application does not collect precise location data such as GPS coordinates.

5. Advertising

The Application may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob. These advertising services may collect information including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Interaction with advertisements

Users can manage or reset their Advertising ID through their device settings.

6. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify users or analyze application performance. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Third-party services used by the Application may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

The Service Provider may share or disclose information in the following situations:

  • Legal Requirements — If required by law or legal process, information may be disclosed to comply with legal obligations.
  • Service Providers — Trusted third-party service providers may process information on behalf of the Service Provider for analytics, advertising, or application functionality.
  • Fraud Prevention and Security — Information may be used to detect abuse, cheating, or fraudulent activities within the Application.

8. Data Retention

User information is retained for as long as necessary to provide the Application's services, and for a reasonable time thereafter.

If you wish to request deletion of your data, you may contact the Service Provider using the email provided in the Contact section below. The Service Provider will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Opt-Out Rights

Users may stop all information collection by uninstalling the Application. Uninstalling the Application can be done using the standard uninstall process available on the mobile device or through the mobile application marketplace.

10. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If it is discovered that a child under 13 has provided personal information, the Service Provider will take steps to delete such information from the servers. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal information should contact the Service Provider so appropriate actions can be taken.

11. Data Security

The Service Provider is committed to protecting user information. Reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards are implemented to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the Application after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

13. Your Consent

By using the Application, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.
