JUDO – Survival

A Strategic Elimination Board Game Where Only One Color Survives.

JUDO – Survival is a strategic elimination-based board game where players compete to survive until only one color remains on the board.

Unlike traditional race-style board games, JUDO focuses on survival and tactical elimination.

Players must outmaneuver opponents, protect their own tokens, and strategically eliminate enemies.

Core Mechanics

  • Strategic token movement
  • Permanent elimination system
  • Dynamic board evolution
  • Multiple gameplay modes
  • Competitive survival gameplay
